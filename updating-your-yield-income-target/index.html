Updating your Yield Income Target

Change your annual yield income goal anytime to reflect your evolving investment strategy.

How to Update Your Target

  1. Navigate to Investor Profile in the main menu

  2. Find the "My Income Goal" section

  3. Click Adjust

  4. Enter your new annual income target (in USD)

  5. Save your changes

Your dashboard will immediately update to show your progress toward the new goal.

How the Yield Income Tracker Works

Your dashboard displays:

Current Annual Run-Rate Your projected annual income based on current daily yield from in-range positions.

Calculation: Daily Yield × 365 days

Your Target The annual income goal you've set (e.g., "$10,000 /year")

Progress Indicator Visual bar showing how close you are to reaching your goal, with the exact amount remaining displayed.

Important: Only in-range positions that are actively earning fees count toward your annual run-rate. Out-of-range positions are excluded since they're not generating yield.

Setting a Realistic Target

Consider your capital If you have $100,000 deployed and target $50,000/year, you'd need to maintain a 50% APR across all positions—which is ambitious.

Account for market conditions Bear markets typically offer lower yields than bull markets. Your target might need adjustment based on overall DeFi market conditions.

Factor in active management Higher targets often require more frequent rebalancing to keep positions in range and maximizing fees.

Start conservative You can always increase your target as your portfolio grows or market opportunities improve.

Why Track Your Income Goal?

Focus your strategy Having a clear target helps you make better decisions about position sizing, protocol selection, and risk management.

Measure progress See at a glance whether your current positions are on track to meet your annual income goals.

Adjust as needed Your financial goals change over time. Update your target as your portfolio grows or your income needs shift.
